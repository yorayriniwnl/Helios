Eye Contact Strategy — Delivery layer

Keep it natural and purposeful: use the screen to show evidence, the panel to show impact.

- Opening (0–10s): Look at the judges. Deliver your first line while making brief eye contact with each person (1–2s).
- Showing a headline or metric: Look at the screen to point (1–3s), then immediately look back at judges and state the implication.
- Walking through steps or remediation: Alternate every 5–8s — glance to the screen to reference, face judges to explain why it matters.
- Q&A or decision moments: Maintain eye contact with the asker; sweep the panel to include everyone when you answer.
- Technical checks: Say "I'll confirm that" and look at the screen for up to 10s; then summarize while looking at judges.
- If the demo breaks: Use the prepared recovery line in docs/demo_failure_recovery.md and keep eye contact while you pivot.

Practice these transitions with a timer until they feel effortless.
