# Slide Deck Outline — Helios (10 slides)

1. Title / Hook
- Title: Helios — Recovering Revenue, Restoring Reliability
- Content: One-line killer hook: "India loses over ₹30,000 crore every year to electricity theft and distribution losses — Helios finds it and turns it back into revenue." Presenter, affiliation, 1-line ask (pilot partner / funding).

2. The Problem — Scale & Impact
- Title: The Revenue Drain
- Content: Short bullets with concrete metrics: AT&C % ranges, estimated national loss (₹ crores), customer & reliability impact (outages, emergency procurement, tariff pressure).

- India‑specific examples to make it real:
	- Summer overload (urban cooling): peak summer demand and overloaded transformers cause higher losses and mask theft signatures — argue for smart short‑horizon forecasts to isolate theft vs load.
	- Night theft (under‑reporting): repeated overnight under‑reporting in residential clusters that only shows up as persistent deviation from baseline at night; stealthy and high ROI when recovered.
	- Dense urban transformers: single transformer feeding dense slums/markets with high non‑technical losses and meter tampering—localizing to transformer/feeder unlocks rapid recovery.

	Suggestion for slide text (one short paragraph):
	"In India, theft hides behind real problems: overloaded transformers in summer, stealthy night‑time bypasses in residential pockets, and dense urban feeders where a single transformer leak can cost crores. We target these patterns directly — forecast the expected load, detect deviations, and send a prioritized inspector pack with audit evidence."

3. Why Now
- Title: Why This Is Urgent
- Content: Rapid load growth (EVs, cooling), aging meters, fiscal stress at DISCOMs; delay increases cumulative losses and blocks modernization.

4. Existing Approaches Fail
- Title: Why Audits & Rules Don’t Scale
- Content: Periodic audits are slow; thresholding is noisy; blanket replacements are capital intensive — list operational pain points and typical false positive / coverage issues.

5. Our Solution — Helios Overview
- Title: Helios — Real-time, Explainable, Operational
- Content: Core capabilities: streaming detection, explainable AI, ROI-driven triage, mobile inspector workflow, mixed‑fleet robustness, auditable evidence capture.

6. How It Works — Data → Decision → Action
- Title: System Flow (simple schematic)
- Content: Ingest (MDMS/AMI/edge) → Pre-check & normalization → Forecasting + anomaly models → ROI scoring & prioritized alerts → Inspector mobile app → Audit & recovery.

7. Demo Highlights (what to show)
- Title: Live Demo Checklist
- Content: 1) Alert list filtered by expected recovered INR; 2) Alert detail with feature attribution & time-series snippet; 3) Mobile evidence capture (photo, geo, signature); 4) Post-inspection verified recovery entry and dashboard KPI update.

8. Pilot Plan & Timeline
- Title: Pilot: 5k meters, 8–24 weeks
- Content: Scope (5k meters, 3–10 feeders), timeline (prep, deploy, validate), success metrics (AT&C delta, recovered INR, precision targets), deliverables (dashboard, inspector app, pilot report).

9. Impact & Economics
- Title: Measurable Outcomes
- Content: Conservative & optimistic scenarios: % theft reduction, gross & net recovered INR (year 1), expected payback at tranche scale; reference to `docs/impact_metrics.md` for calculations.

10. Ask & Next Steps
- Title: Ask — Pilot Partner & Resources
- Content: Clear ask (pilot funding, DISCOM intro, data access), immediate next steps (MoU, SOW, 0–3 month sprint), contact and team slide note.
