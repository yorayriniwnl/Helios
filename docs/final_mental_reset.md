Final Mental Reset — Clarity · Confidence · Control

Take 60 seconds to clear your mind and focus before you start.

Step 1 — Breathe (0–15s)
- Sit or stand tall. Do three slow breaths: inhale 4s, hold 2s, exhale 6s.

Step 2 — One-line focus (15–30s)
- Silently repeat: "Clarity: say the headline. Confidence: slow, steady voice. Control: backup ready."

Step 3 — Visualize (30–45s)
- Run the demo in your head: opener → metric → remediation → close, smoothly and calmly.

Step 4 — Tech & posture check (45–60s)
- Confirm `docs/silent_demo_play.html` or your recording is ready. Relax shoulders, smile, look forward.

Final: Say the opening line once aloud, pause 1s, breathe, then begin.

Notes:
- Keep this routine brief and consistent before every run.
- If nerves spike, take one deep breath and whisper the micro-affirmation: "Fix fast."
