# If the demo breaks — what to say immediately

Pick one short line and deliver it calmly, then act on the backup.

- Confident pivot (recommended): "Thanks — we just hit a small hiccup. I have a short backup that shows the exact flow; I'll play it now so we don't lose time."
- Quick restart: "I'll restart the demo and be back in 30 seconds — thanks for your patience." 
- Redirect to recording: "To keep us on schedule, I'll switch to a 60‑second recording that shows the full end‑to‑end flow."

Keep tone: pause 1s, smile, speak clearly. Do not over-apologize or over-explain.

Immediate actions (do these silently):

- Open the backup: `docs/silent_demo_play.html` or the recorded 60s demo video.
- If needed, seed demo data or switch to the recorded playback and fullscreen it.
- If a live restart is chosen, say the quick restart line and start a timed 30s reset.

Short answer if asked "why": "This is a transient environment hiccup; everything is reproducible and logged — the backup shows the correct behavior." 

Follow-up offer: "I'll send the recording and logs right after this session and can run a live replay on demand."
