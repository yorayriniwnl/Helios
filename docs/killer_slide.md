# Catch Theft. Recover Revenue. Restore Trust.

Real-time detection + surgical inspections = recover revenue fast.

**Problem**
- Hidden theft and tampering create sustained revenue loss.
- Data volume dwarfs manual triage: 100k meters @15-min cadence -> ~9.6M reads/day.
- Manual inspection is slow, unfocused and expensive.

**Impact**
- Targeted inspections recover lost revenue and reduce wasted field effort.
- Pilot: measurable recoveries and a repeatable playbook within 3-6 months.
- Outcome: faster recovery, better collections, and restored trust.

**Solution**
- Automated anomaly detection across time-series plus photo-backed evidence.
- Hybrid rules + ML with confidence scoring gates enforcement to high-value cases.
- Stateless APIs, autoscaled workers and partitioned time-series storage for horizontal scale.

**Call to action**
- Pilot: 5k meters, 3 months -> validated recovery and a replicable scaling plan.

**Tagline**
- "Find the theft you can fix — fast."
